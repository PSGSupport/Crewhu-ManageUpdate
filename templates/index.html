Welcome to Crewhu Integration

Follow the three simple steps below to sync your Crewhu survey data with ConnectWise Manage. Each step builds on the previous one, so complete them in order.

1
First Step

Parse Crewhu Emails

Upload your exported Crewhu notification emails (JSON format) to extract survey data including ratings, feedback, and ticket numbers.

💡 How to get your email export:

  • Run the PowerShell script emailParse.ps1 on a machine with Outlook
  • Or export emails manually as JSON from your email client
  • The file should contain Crewhu notification emails

Second Step

Update Ticket Ratings

Upload your Crewhu ratings CSV export to update the "Latest Crewhu Rating" custom field on ConnectWise tickets.

💡 What you need:

  • Export your survey history from Crewhu as CSV
  • CSV must have "Ticket#" and "Rating" columns
  • Ratings will be mapped: AWESOME/POSITIVE → "Positive", NEGATIVE → "Negative"
